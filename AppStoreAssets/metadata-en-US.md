## Description

Snack Swap Adventure is a bright match-3 puzzle game built for quick, satisfying sessions.

Swap tasty snacks to clear the board, feed your monster mascot, and chase higher scores across a colorful world map. Every level gives you a new objective, from collecting specific treats to creating combos and racing against the clock.

Features:

- Fast match-3 snack swapping
- Timed levels with extra-time rewards
- Sugar Rush combo meter
- Animated monster mascot
- World map progression
- Star rewards and replayable levels
- Optional star packs for extra timer saves

Jump in, make a match, and keep the monsters fed.
